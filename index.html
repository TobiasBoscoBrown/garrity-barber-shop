The Garrity way

The kind of shop folks call a secret

Big chains rush you through a number system and hand you a bill that makes you wince. Garrity is the other thing. You get a chair, a real barber who remembers how you like it, a little conversation if you want it, and a clean cut that holds its shape. Around here people still say it is the best shop since the late, legendary Razor Ray. We take that as the highest compliment in town.

Real barbers, real chairs

Cindy, Tammy, Jake and Shannon have decades between them. With several chairs going, the wait stays short.

Honest prices

Cuts start at $16, kids and seniors pay less, and a beard trim is $7. One regular called it fifty dollars of care for sixteen bucks.

Walk right in

No app to download, no appointment to book. Pop in off 2nd Street Tuesday through Saturday and grab the next open chair.

The chairs

Run by barbers who remember your name

Garrity has been part of downtown Nampa for more than thirty years, and the regulars treat it like a second living room. Cindy, Tammy, Jake and Shannon work the chairs, and folks drive in from out of town and even out of state just to sit in one of them. Customers have moved away and still make the hundred mile trip back for a cut they trust.

It is the kind of place where there is barber shop talk in the air, kids get their first cut, and old timers swap stories. No frills, no pretense, just steady hands and a fair price, the way a neighborhood barbershop is supposed to be.

Good to know

Do I need an appointment?+

No. Garrity is a walk-in shop. Just come in during open hours and take the next available chair. If you are short on time, give us a call at (208) 467-2331 to check the wait.

How much is a haircut?+

A standard cut is $16, long hair is $18, and a skin fade is $20. Kids are $15, seniors $14, and a beard trim is $7. Color, bleach and perm services are available too. Cash and card are both fine.

Where are you located?+

311 2nd Street South in downtown Nampa, Idaho. Look for the brick storefront with the barber pole and the Garrity Barber Shop sign. There is street parking right out front.

Do you cut kids' hair?+

Absolutely. We are a family friendly shop and kids' cuts are $15. Plenty of first haircuts have happened in these chairs.

What days are you open?+

Tuesday through Friday from 9am to 5pm, and Saturday from 9am to 2pm. We are closed Sunday and Monday.

Ready for a fresh cut?

Swing by 2nd Street, or call ahead and we will tell you how busy the chairs are. We would love to see you.
